About

BVL Support is a collection of interactive tools built for neuroscientists and EEG researchers. Whether you're working with Brain Products hardware or just need reliable utilities for your lab, this is a growing hub of practical resources.

Made by Biraj Shrestha — a Technical Support Engineer at Brain Vision who works with these systems every day. These tools come from real problems encountered in the field: trigger decoding, signal testing, calibration, and quality control.

Have a suggestion or found something useful? Reach out at support@brainvision.com.
